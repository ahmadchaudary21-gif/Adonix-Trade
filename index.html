What We Do

Adonix Trade is a commodity trading company specialising in the physical sourcing, trading, and logistics of raw materials across international markets. We bridge the gap between producers and end-users, ensuring reliable supply at competitive terms across all major commodity categories.

Global Sourcing

Identifying and securing high-quality commodity supplies from producers worldwide across all major categories.

Logistics & Trade Execution

Managing end-to-end supply chains from origin to destination with full documentation and freight coordination.

Risk Management

Hedging strategies and financial instruments to protect clients against commodity price volatility.

Market Intelligence

Real-time pricing analysis and global market insight to support informed strategic commodity decisions.

Trade Finance

Letters of credit, structured finance, and working capital solutions for physical commodity transactions.
